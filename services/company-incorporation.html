Steps to Incorporation

The incorporation process involves several key steps:

edit_note

1. Name Reservation

Reserve your business name with the SEC.

description

2. Document Submission

Submit Articles of Incorporation and By-Laws to SEC.

verified

3. Secure Permits

Obtain Barangay Clearance, Mayor’s Permit, and Business Permit.

After securing these permits, you will need to register with the Bureau of Internal Revenue for corporate taxation and obtain necessary certifications from SSS, PhilHealth, and Pag-IBIG Fund if you plan to employ individuals.
