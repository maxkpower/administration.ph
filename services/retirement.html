Retirement Services

The Philippines offers an attractive retirement program for individuals over 50 years old, providing various options to suit different needs and preferences. With a modest pension or capital deposit, retirees can enjoy the benefits of living in the Philippines, including beautiful landscapes, a friendly community, and affordable living costs.

Our retirement services help you navigate the requirements and processes to secure your Special Resident Retiree’s Visa (SRRV). We provide assistance with paperwork, local banking, healthcare options, and real estate investments, ensuring a smooth transition into your retirement life in the Philippines.

We understand that retiring in a new country can be a daunting process. Our dedicated team is here to make it as seamless as possible, providing you with the support and guidance needed every step of the way.

SRRV Categories

There are several SRRV categories to choose from, each with specific requirements:

sentiment_very_satisfied

SRRV Smile

For active and healthy retirees over 50 with a US$20,000 deposit.

real_estate_agent

SRRV Classic

For retirees who wish to invest in real estate with a deposit of US$10,000-$20,000.

healing

SRRV Human Touch

For ailing retirees over 50 requiring medical care, with a US$10,000 deposit.

Explore these options and find the one that best suits your retirement plans. With our comprehensive services, you can enjoy a fulfilling and worry-free retirement in the Philippines.
